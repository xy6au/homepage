Chinatown

Founded: 1850s

Population: 3,425

Area: 6th Street to 12th Street, Broadway to Fallon Street

Study about environment.

The wind, the light, the green space, the pollution.

When I went to Oakland, I recorded the wind speed in different areas and in different levels. Then I reorded a video about what is going on in downtown and Chinatown in one day. I also built a wind model that analyse how wind blows through the blocks.

Programs

Chinatown has a lot of good restaurants, bars and markets, at the same time, there are parking lots in Chinatown which reduce the area of green space.

Light analysis

Buildings in Chinatown are too dense to have enough light in the rooms and in the yard.The small green spaces in the blocks need more open and get more lights.

Pollution

Pollution from the highway and the streets is easy to accumulate between the buildings and in the small green spaces.
