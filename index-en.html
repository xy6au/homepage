ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT

Gas emissions of Oakland.

In this map, we can tell the two highways are the darkest in color, which means the gas emission and air pollution are heaviest in these areas. In addition to these two roads, the pollution in the center of the city is also more serious. The main reasons of that are the vehicle flowrate and speed of a motor vehicle.

Wind and sun path.

Oakland is mainly westerly, and the wind is very strong.As the model showing, the wind speed in the middle of the building slows down and pollutants are easily deposited in these places.
